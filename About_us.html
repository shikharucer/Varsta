Shipping Information

Our goal is to offer you the best shipping options, no matter where you live.

The time frame for order delivery is divided into two parts:

1.Processing time: Order verification, tailoring, quality check and packaging. All orders are sent to the manufacturer for dispatch within 24 hours after the order is placed.

2.Shipping Time: After processing and leaving the warehouse, items usually take between 7 and 14 days to arrive at their destination.
